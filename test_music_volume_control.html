Music Volume Control Widget Test

This test demonstrates the music volume control widget for non-DM users. The widget allows users to adjust their personal volume and mute music without affecting other players.

Information

How it works:

  • User Volume: Personal volume multiplier (stored in localStorage)
  • DM Volume: Base volume set by the DM (from server)
  • Effective Volume: DM Volume × User Volume (applied to audio element)
  • Mute: Temporarily sets effective volume to 0 without affecting saved preferences
  • Widget Visibility: Only shows when music is playing
